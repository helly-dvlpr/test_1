Who

The Company LETAP PRINTPACK was incorporated in the year 1994, about three decades ago, in a humble way with a view of assisting the packing material in the State of Gujarat. It is a matter of pride that with the expertise and sincere efforts put in by the Founder, Mr. N.K. Patel, who is Graduate in B.SC (CHEMISTRY) with a remarkable practical experience of over 40 years, the Company has turned out to be a Group of Companies in a short span of time.

We are one of the well-known entrepreneurs, engaged in the manufacturing of packaging material with a proven track record on quality of products, trouble-free performance.
